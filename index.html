BERGEN COUNTY ACADEMIES

Welcome to the Bergen County Academies Admissions Site!

The Bergen County Academies is a free public magnet high school of choice for Bergen County Residents located on the Dr. John Greico Campus in Hackensack, New Jersey. We offer students a unique high school experience that combines comprehensive academics with technical and professional courses. We encourage our students to choose an academy concentration based upon their interests rather than a future career choice.


Pursue your passion for science, art, computers, music, theatre, cooking, design, or finance-our programs will allow you to explore your interest while preparing you to meet the academic challenge of college. You may complement your studies with electives and clubs that cross academy boundaries; you are not limited to study in just one field. Students receive a fine academic background that prepares them well for postsecondary study in any field they choose.

Accolades

  • Named a 2015 National Blue Ribbon School by the U.S. Department of Education.
  • Recognized as the #5 School in the Nation by Newsweek 2015.
  • Home of six Intel Science Talent Search Finalists in the past six years.
  • Home of a member of the 2015 U.S. International Math Olympiad Team.

If you would like to apply to the Bergen County Academies:


  1. 1. You must be a resident of Bergen County when you complete your application.
  2. 2. You must be in the 8th grade by September 2016.
  3. 3. If accepted you will being your 9th grade year in September 2017; we do not accept transfer students or upperclassmen.
  • The Bergen County Academies in Hackensack, New Jersey was among the 285 public and 50 private schools nationwide to be recognized as 2015 National Blue Ribbon Schools. Bergen County Technical Schools Superintendent Dr. Howard Lerner, BCA Principal Mr. Russell Davis, and Mr. William Pavlu represented the school at a gala ceremony in Washington, D.C on November 9 and 10, 2015. Bergen County Academies was awarded Blue Ribbon status in the category of Exemplary High Performing Schools, a designation that acknowledges BCA as one of New Jersey's highest performing schools as measured by state assessments or nationally normed tests, student performance, and high school graduation rates.
