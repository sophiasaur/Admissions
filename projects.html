Projects

During their first three years at the Academies, students will spend two hours every Wednesday working on an interdisciplinary project. Students will have the opportunity to choose a different project each trimester. Some aspects of the projects are fairly consistent:

  • Using the internet to research a topic
  • Working in more than one discipline
  • Working as part of a team
  • Making a final presentation

    • Students can choose among the available teacher-designed projects. Projects include:
