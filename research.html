Applying to the Academies

If you are interested into applying to Bergen County Academies:

  1. You must be in the 8th grade by September 2016
  2. You must be a resident of Bergen County when you submit your online application.
  3. If accepted you will begin your 9th grade year in September 2017; we do not accept transfer students or upperclassmen.
